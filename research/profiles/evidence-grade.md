# Evidence-grade research profile

Optional profile for governance, regulation, vendor selection, externally
published market claims, or expensive decisions resting on contested facts.
This preserves the original research-team method; it is not the default for
incremental product discovery.

## Lifecycle

1. Plan independent workstreams and non-overlapping source-ID blocks.
2. Investigate in parallel.
3. Merge proposed sources into the structured ledger.
4. Identify material claims and run the evidence verifier.
5. Apply disputes, downgrades and retractions under the evidence contract.
6. Synthesise a draft decision brief.
7. Run contrarian review.
8. Answer every material challenge and finalise.

## Required contracts

- `research/core/evidence-contract.md`
- `research/core/source-ledger.md`
- `ledger.md`, `verification.md`, `contrarian-review.md`, and `brief.md` in
  the mission directory.

## Cost discipline

- Define a source budget in the mission rather than searching without a stop
  condition.
- Verify material claims only.
- Resume from checkpoints; do not repeat completed phases.
- Finalisation edits the checkpointed draft and opens underlying findings
  only when a challenge requires them.

## Decision brief

1. Context.
2. What survived verification.
3. Recommendations mapped to evidence and constraints.
4. What not to adopt.
5. Open hypotheses and validation actions.
6. Every contrarian challenge and response.
7. Suggested next steps.

The brief informs a human decision and never claims the decision itself.
